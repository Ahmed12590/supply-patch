WHO WE ARE

A Cleaner Way to Buy Inventory For Wholesale and Liquidation Buyers

Supply Patch connects real buyers with brand-name wholesale, closeout and liquidation inventory. We work with retailers, Amazon sellers, Walmart sellers, marketplace resellers and volume buyers who want clear lot details and inventory that fits how they sell.

Wholesale and liquidation buying can be messy. Lots move fast and the details are not always clear. We give serious buyers a simpler way to review brand-name opportunities, with clear category and condition information before they buy.

Clear Lot Details

Every opportunity includes the information that matters, including product type, quantity, condition notes and overall lot fit. Buyers can review what is actually in a lot before making a decision.

Brand Name Opportunities

We focus on recognizable brand-name inventory across beauty, electronics, home, toys, grocery, household goods and tools. Buyers get access to categories their customers already know.

Channel-Aware Inventory

Opportunities are reviewed with each sales channel in mind, including Amazon, Walmart, Whatnot, retail and resale. Buyers can focus on products that match how they actually sell.

Inventory Submission Review

Vendors, suppliers and inventory holders can submit excess stock, closeouts, mixed lots or full catalogs for review by our buying team.
