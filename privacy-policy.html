What we collect.

When you contact us, request a quote, submit an inquiry, or subscribe to updates, we collect the information you voluntarily provide, including your name, business name, email address, phone number, and details about your inventory or wholesale requirements.

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request.

How we use your information.

Any information you submit through our website is stored securely and used only for legitimate business purposes, such as responding to inquiries, processing requests, providing inventory updates, and improving our customer service.

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties except when necessary to provide our services or when required by applicable law.
