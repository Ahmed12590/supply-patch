COMMON QUESTIONS

Answers Before You Buy

Quick answers about order size, lot details, buyer channels and how Supply Patch works.

What are the minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product, lot type and supplier. Each opportunity includes its own quantity and purchase requirements.

Do you provide condition manifests?

When available, manifests, product lists, quantities and condition information are shared before the purchase is confirmed.

Which channels do you serve?

We work with retailers, wholesalers, Amazon sellers, Walmart sellers, eBay sellers, Whatnot sellers and other ecommerce businesses.

How fast can inventory be ready?

Lead times depend on inventory location, payment, freight arrangements and lot preparation requirements.

Can I sell excess inventory to Supply Patch?

Yes. You can submit your product catalog, quantities, condition details and expected pricing for review by our buying team.
